About Wrights Bake House

Creating beautiful cakes in Stowmarket since 2020

Meet Lauren

Welcome to Wrights Bake House! I'm Lauren, a passionate cake maker based in Stowmarket, dedicated to creating beautiful and delicious cakes for all your special occasions.

Every cake is made with love, care, and the finest ingredients. From birthday cakes to wedding cakes, I work closely with you to bring your cake dreams to life.

Our Cake Services

Birthday Cakes

Make every birthday extra special with our custom birthday cakes. From children's themed cakes to elegant adult celebrations, we create the perfect cake for your special day.

Wedding Cakes

Your wedding day deserves a cake as beautiful as your love story. Our wedding cakes are crafted with elegance and taste, making your special day even more memorable.

Vegan Cakes

Delicious doesn't have to mean dairy! Our vegan cakes are just as tasty and beautiful as traditional cakes, perfect for those with dietary preferences or allergies.

Fondant Cakes

Transform your cake into a work of art with our fondant designs. From simple elegance to elaborate sculptures, we can create any design you imagine.

Local Delivery

Convenient cake delivery throughout Stowmarket and surrounding areas. Your beautiful cake will arrive safely and on time for your special occasion.

Custom Designs

Have a specific vision in mind? We love creating custom cake designs! Share your ideas and we'll bring them to life with our creative expertise.
