Food Allergy Disclaimer

Important Notice

These are homemade cakes that are NOT made in an allergy-free kitchen. I cannot guarantee that the products I make are free from ingredients that may affect those with food allergies.

Liability Statement

I recognise the seriousness of food allergies but cannot assume any liability for adverse reactions to my products.

Ingredients Used in My Kitchen

The following ingredients are used in my kitchen where the products are made:

  • Milk and other dairy products
  • Eggs
  • Nuts: all variations
  • Wheat and gluten

Food Coloring Notice

Please note that some food colourings may have an effect on behaviour in children.

Customer Responsibility

It is your responsibility to:

  • Inform us of any allergy issues prior to booking confirmation
  • Inform your guests about potential allergens
  • Make informed decisions about consuming our products
  • Seek medical advice if you have concerns about food allergies
